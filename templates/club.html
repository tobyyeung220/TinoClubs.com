Black Student Union

#Cultural Club #Everyone is Welcome to Join #Meeting this Tuesday #Black Lives Matter #Student Union

The Purpose of Black Student Union is to:

  1. Provide a safe space for everyone on our campus, with a heavy emphasis on the little population of black students on our campus
  2. Inform, educate, and advise Cupertino High, as well as our community, on issues that heavily affect the black community

BSU is a judgment-free, safe, validating environment, where mental health, self-acceptance, and acknowledgment are made to prosper.
